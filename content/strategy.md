Sapiens Alpha's India Fund of Funds employs a systematic, rules-based quantitative process to construct a portfolio of Indian mutual funds benchmarked against the BSE 500.

Starting from a screened universe of direct-plan equity and flexi-cap schemes, the model applies a scoring framework to identify four uncorrelated funds. The portfolio maximises risk-adjusted alpha while constraining overall volatility and drawdown. Robustness is validated through rigorous out-of-sample forward simulations.

This Balanced Architecture approach has historically delivered a 2.32% annualised alpha over the BSE 500, with 4.05% lower volatility and a 6.48% shallower maximum drawdown, rebalanced annually across the 2020–25 simulation period.
